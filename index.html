A National Level Coding Contest
About

Technocrats Institute of Technology presents a national level programming contest, 'CUBG-Coder Unknown's BattleGround' Powered by Panaceaedu. It is a 10 hour non-stop programming event comprising of tech talks, food, rejuvenating fun activities and so on.It is a challenge designed to inspire the creative and dynamic technophiles to put their skills to test, thereby identifying and rewarding the tech wizards of India.

   CUBG invites all programmers and hackathon enthusiasts to participate in an interesting and engaging programming event.

Understanding team formation and registration process
